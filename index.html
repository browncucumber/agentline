FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How accurate is the AI?

Our AI is trained directly from your listing data, making it highly accurate for property-specific questions. It answers based on real facts from your MLS or Zillow link.

Does it actually book showings?

Yes. The AI syncs with your Google Calendar, checks availability in real time, and confirms bookings with callers automatically — no manual input required.

Can it answer property questions?

Absolutely. Pricing, square footage, bedroom count, open house dates, HOA info — it handles all standard buyer inquiries without you lifting a finger.

Does it work with Zillow listings?

Yes. Simply paste your Zillow or MLS listing URL and we extract all the property information needed to train your custom AI assistant.

How do I start the free trial?

Click any "Start Free Trial" button, choose your plan, and follow the setup steps. You'll be live with your AI number within minutes.

What happens after the trial?

You'll be automatically billed at your plan's monthly rate unless you cancel before the trial ends. No hidden fees, no setup charges.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All call data is processed through enterprise-grade AI infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. We never sell or share your data.

Can I add multiple listings?

Yes — each plan supports multiple listings. Starter handles 1–2, Pro handles up to 3, and Team handles up to 5 active listings simultaneously.

Does it integrate with Google Calendar?

Yes. Google Calendar integration is included on all plans. The AI checks your real-time availability and adds booked showings automatically.

How fast can I get set up?

Most agents are fully live in under 5 minutes. Paste your listing link, connect your calendar, and your AI number is ready to share immediately.
